Description

A Bloody Valentine's is a two track Valentine's Day CD single by Krack Hed, released through Krazy Records in 2026. The release contains the title track featuring Proven Sawlid along with a bonus track also matching the valentine's day theme, this track is titled "Steppin' 2 U."

A Bloody Valentine's [Valentines's Special 2026] album cover shows a Pink hue with the Drawing of Krack Hed from the Beaten Bloody Ep shown on the Cover, Along with the text "A Bloody Valentine's" in a cursive like font. And a Valentines day background scenery to match the theme of the Album, "A Bloody Valentine's" was Krack Hed's first ever Collab with an artist, "Proven Sawlid" was the feature on the track "A Bloody Valentine's" and "Proven Sawlid is a big part of who i am today and he should be shown respect, and if you got a problem with him then you got a problem with me aswell Muthafucka." - Krack Hed.
