Description

The Somethin' 2 Say is a two track CD single by the Krazy Krack Hed, released through Krazy Records in 2026. The release contains the title track "Somethin' 2 Say" along with a bonus track, this track is titled "Ol' School Ryde"

The Art on the Album cover shows a Character which most people would assume that its Krack Hed, it is not and its Actually one of Krack Hed's Younger Brother "Max", which goes by "Rebel Max" or "Rednek Max". Rebel Max was going to be originally Featured on to this Track but he later dropped out of the track near the end. The Image of the cover was later changed and tweaked a bit and things that made the character "Rebel Max" were removed to not have him mentioned on the track and seem thats just a random character. And thats not a reason to get upset with Rebel Max the track was still released just a whole bunch of parts of that track were edited and Rewritten. The unedited version is currently unreleased But in a chat with friends he was mentioning how he might release the unedited version on a Album for his unreleased tracks.
