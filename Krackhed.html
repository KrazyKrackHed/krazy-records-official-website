KRACK HED

Biography

Krack Hed is a horrorcore rapper and founder of Krazy Records.

History

The 4 Hobo'$ (2024)

The Krazy Krack Hed you know today wasn't always known as "Krack Hed." Before adopting that name, he experimented with several different aliases, including "Jugga-Boe", "BoedaJuggalo", and simply "Hobo." Eventually he settled on the name BoeDaHobo, marking the beginning of his music career. Wanting to create music with others instead of working alone, BoeDaHobo formed a small rap group with his three younger brothers titled The 4 Hobo'$. During this time he began experimenting with different styles while slowly developing the horrorcore sound that would later define his music. His influences included Insane Clown Posse (ICP), Esham the Unholy, Twiztid, and Dark Lotus. Besides writing music, BoeDaHobo also spent much of his time designing original artwork and creating album concepts. Although the group had many ideas for future projects, The 4 Hobo'$ lasted only around six months before falling apart, ending the group's short-lived run.

The Halloween Killaz (Late 2024-Present)

Near the end of 2024, BoeDaHobo changed his stage name to Krack Hed and founded the horrorcore hip-hop group The Halloween Killaz (T.H.K.) alongside Stitch-Face Sam, Voodoo Bastard, and Hollow-Head. Although the group planned multiple projects, Krack Hed was the only member consistently creating music and artwork, causing the group to slowly become inactive. Eventually Voodoo Bastard and Hollow-Head left the group, leaving Krack Hed and Stitch-Face Sam as the remaining members. As of 2026, the duo continues to keep The Halloween Killaz alive, with plans to release music every Fall and Halloween season. Their current project is the upcoming LP Halloween Horror, featuring original songs, remixes, and classic Halloween-inspired horrorcore themes.

Krazy Records (2026-Present)

In 2026, Krack Hed founded Krazy Records, an independent horrorcore hip-hop record label created to release, archive, and preserve his music. The label became home to projects including Krazy Mix Volume 1, A Bloody Valentine's, Somethin' 2 Say, and future releases. Along with releasing music digitally, Krack Hed also began producing limited physical Compact Disc (CD) releases while developing the official Krazy Records website as a complete archive for the label's artists and discography.

Wack Muzik (2026-Present)

During 2026, Krack Hed established Wack Muzik, a sub-label operating under Krazy Records. The purpose of Wack Muzik is to separate certain projects from the main Krazy Records catalog while still remaining part of the overall label family. The first release issued through Wack Muzik was Golden Rhymez, introducing a different direction from Krack Hed's traditional horrorcore releases. Showing a "Wannabe Wangsta" type style on the Ep.

Present

Today, Krack Hed continues recording music while expanding both Krazy Records and Wack Muzik. He plans to revisit older projects, remaster previous releases, continue producing physical CD editions, and further expand the official Krazy Records website. Current projects include the upcoming Juggalo-exclusive album Chop! Chop! Chop! and future releases by both Krack Hed and The Halloween Killaz. His long-term goal is to preserve every release, artist, and project through the Krazy Records archive while continuing to create new music.
